Absolute vs Relative Position in VLOOKUP vs XLOOKUP

VLOOKUP and XLOOKUP differ fundamentally in how they locate values: VLOOKUP requires absolute positioning (counting columns from left) while XLOOKUP uses relative positioning (searching for exact match then returning adjacent value). This distinction determines which function is appropriate for dynamic range scenarios and leftward lookups.
