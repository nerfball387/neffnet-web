SCAN function for iterative calculations in Excel

The SCAN function enables iterative calculations across arrays, allowing complex transformations and financial modeling in a single cell formula. It works with LAMBDA to perform cumulative operations where each result feeds into the next calculation, eliminating the need for helper columns or complex array logic.
