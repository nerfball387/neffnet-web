REPT Function: In-Cell Mini Bar Charts with One Formula

Use REPT("█", value) to render horizontal bar charts directly inside cells — no chart object required. Pairs well with conditional formatting for quick visual dashboards. A low-barrier technique worth showing students as an alternative to full chart builds.
