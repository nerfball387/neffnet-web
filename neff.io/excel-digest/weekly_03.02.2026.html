Structured References: Mindset Shift from Position-Driven to Name-Driven Objects

Table structured references leverage named ranges within Excel tables rather than cell positions, reducing complexity when building formulas that reference table data. The technique involves understanding that Excel tables are fundamentally name-driven objects, not position-driven, which clarifies formula construction and maintenance.
