Preventing Conditional Formatting Rule Duplication with Relative References

When conditional formatting rules use relative references (e.g., =A4=A3), inserting rows causes Excel to adjust those references, creating duplicate and fragmented rules in the Rules Manager. This occurs because Excel treats conditional formatting relative references identically to formula references, requiring either absolute references or rule restructuring to maintain integrity.
