Disconnected Table Slicer Technique

Uses slicers independently of PivotTables by connecting them to a disconnected helper table, enabling interactive filtering for formula-driven calculations on regular data ranges. The technique separates slicer controls from pivot functionality, allowing dynamic filtering across any dataset using FILTER, INDEX/MATCH, or SUMIF formulas referenced to slicer selections.
